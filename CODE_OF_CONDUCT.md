# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want Linmas to be a respectful, safe, and constructive open-source project.
Contributors, maintainers, and community members should participate without harassment or abuse.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that support a positive environment include:

- being respectful and professional;
- giving and receiving constructive feedback;
- focusing on defensive, authorized, and safety-conscious security work;
- showing empathy toward other community members.

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks;
- publishing private information without permission;
- encouraging unauthorized access, harmful exploitation, malware, stealth, persistence, or evasion;
- disruptive, deceptive, or bad-faith participation.

## Enforcement Responsibilities

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, issues, commits, code, wiki edits, and other contributions that violate this Code of Conduct.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within project spaces and in public spaces when someone is representing the project or its community.

## Reporting

Please report Code of Conduct concerns privately to the maintainers.
For security-sensitive reports, follow `.github/SECURITY.md` instead.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may take any action they deem appropriate, including warning, temporary restriction, or permanent removal from project spaces.
